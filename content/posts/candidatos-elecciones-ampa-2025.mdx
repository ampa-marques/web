These are the people who have submitted their candidacy for the AMPA Board of the Primer Marqués del Turia Primary School.

We remind you that the vote will take place **next Friday, December 5th, from 9:00 to 11:00 and from 15:30 to 17:00** at the school.

Each member may vote for one candidate (one vote per family, regardless of the number of children enrolled at the school). Don't forget to bring your ID card/residence permit/passport.
